Live interpretation of thunderbolts in Greece.


This is a hybrid location based application, part of my Thesis, that is designed to illustrate the activity of lightning in the territory of Greece in real time. Technologies used are Apache cordova, Ionic and AngularJS frameworks, HTML5 and Javascript. Currently supports Greek and English.

Data are recorded and processed by Harokopeio University only for educational purposes and may not be exact.
